about me

Hello, I am Hithesh Shekar and I am a wireless enthusiast. When I’m not immersed in the latest developments on base stations and RAN architecture, I’m likely reading up on the next big thing in 5G and 6G. I enjoy diving into the details of how these networks impact our world and what the future holds for wireless tech. Whether it's analyzing throughput, debugging network layers, or just staying on top of emerging standards, I’m here to connect the world—one innovation at a time.
