Research and Ongoing work

OTIC (Open Testing and Integration Centres)

Equipped with state-of-the-art testing devices from industry leaders like Keysight and Viavi, the Open6G OTIC provides capabilities for validating disaggregated base stations, RAN Intelligent Controllers (RICs), and custom applications (xApps and rApps). This infrastructure supports testing against diverse RAN implementations, including both commercial and software-defined products, in emulated or over-the-air environments

Programmable 5G

Developing open, programmable, and virtualized 5G networks, focusing on network slicing and scalability for diverse applications like IoT and smart cities.

O-RAN + IoT

Advancing integration of IoT with O-RAN to support massive IoT deployments and create seamless, scalable communication systems.
